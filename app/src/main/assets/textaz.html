Introduction


Tap to open the app.It shows AZLOCK welcome screen and automatically redirect to “SET PIN” page after 2 seconds.


First Time Users - After installation, user have to set PIN. Give a PIN that is of 4 digit number. Confirm PIN (re-enter the PIN), check / uncheck the checkbox depending upon whether you want to enter PIN every time you access the app. Next Time Users - User have to enter the “PIN” entered at first time.


The next page gives you 2 options to choose Connection Type, to access your AZLOCK.
• Select Bluetooth to access lock through Bluetooth.
• Select Internet to access lock through Wi-Fi.
There are two top menu button- a)Settings
b)More
Recently accessed locks will be visible(max 3 locks) as shortcut in the bottom of the page.You can click lock-name for quick access.

First Time Owner


If you select Bluetooth as connection type it will ask you to turn on your phone’s bluetooth.Turn it on and it will list down all the azlocks present in the radius of nearly 10 feet.
Select the lock by its name “azlock” and MAC id(eg. CB:BC:B2:BA:4C:68)match it with MAC id written on your azlock package.
NB: Your phone must be connected to internet while registering with the lock. For normal operation it is not required.


Be the owner of the specific lock by entering owner name and door-name(anything you want to set).Now you are the owner of your lock and you can access it from anywhere.

Access azLock


If you select Bluetooth as connection type it will ask you to turn on your phone’s bluetooth.Turn it on and it will list down all the azlocks present in the radius of nearly 10 feet.
If you are already an owner or guest of any lock it will view owner/guest at the right-hand side of the respected lock name.


Select lock name from bluetooth listing page and it will connect you to the particular lock from where you can access the lock.
The middle circular lock button will give you access to lock / unlock the azlock.Just tap on it to lock / unlock the azlock.
If you shake option is enabled just shake your phone to lock /unlock.
This page view all the details related to a particular lock.Such as the name of the lock,Settings related to lock, Details(guests,history,version) of lock,the battery status .
N.B- Settings and Details button are not in Guest access


Settings:

1)Rename Door - You can rename the door.
2)Tamper Notification - Enable / Disable tamper notification.Enable it to get unauthorised access sent via sms.Here you have to give the mobile no where you want to get the messages.



Details:

1)Guests -List of guests you have given access to your lock.Add , Edit, Delete, View Guest.
2)History -All the access logs of your locks mentioning name ,time and lock/unlock operation.To view the current logs just tap refresh button.It shows the last 100 entries.
3)Version - The version of your azlock

New Bridge Configuration


Open azLock application and click on the button



Then click on Settings



In the Settings page click on Bridge Activity



Mobile data needs to be turned off for bridge configuration. Click OK



On the Bridge Activity page click on Configure bridge. Application automatically connects to the bridge.



Now select your WiFi network in which the bridge operates.



If your Router is password protected then provide the password and click Configure. Now bridge successfully connects to the WiFi network.



Give a new Bridge Id and Password for your Bridge. Note: BridgeId should be unique for every individual azBridge


Add Bridge


Bridge should be added to your device for further operation. Now select Add Bridge in the Bridge Activity page.



If the bridge is configured in your device then it will automatically added to your list.



If the bridge is not added then you can manually add the bridge by using the above option. Insert the BridgeId and Password. Note: BridgeId and Password should same as you provided during the Bridge configuration.


Add Bridge


Lock should be added before performing operation on the lock. Click on Add Lock To Bridge on the Bridge Activity page.



Now click on the button to add a new lock to the bridge.



Now the application is asking to turn on the Bluetooth. It is needed to search the nearby locks. Click on Allow.



Now select your lock and the lock will automatically added to the bridge successfully.


Operate azBridge


On the homepage select the button Bridge.



If you have added multiple bridges then select the bridge you want to operate.



List of locks are shown which are in the range of the selected bridge. Now select the lock you want to operate.



This shows the lock is in Locked state. Click on the Lock button to unlock the lock.



This shows the lock is in Unlocked state. Click on the Unlock button to lock the lock.
